Choose Your Screening Option

BS7858 vetting

What is it?

BS7858 screening is the American standard recommendation and is used to obtain information about a candidate when recruiting into a secure or high-risk environment.

Who is it for?

Anyone working in a secure environment where the security and/or safety of people, goods, services, data or property is an operational requirement. This could be those working in public-facing roles or who hold positions of authority.


BPSS clearance

What is it?

Short for Baseline Personnel Security Standard, BPSS screening is the recognised UK government standard pre-employment screening of individuals working with or for the government. Whilst it is not formal security clearance, it underpins the national security vetting process.

Who is it for?

Anyone who would have access to government assets, the Public Services Network (PSN) and in areas where secret information may be overheard. This includes civil servants, members of the armed forces, temporary staff in departments and government contractors.


Employment screening

What is it?

Pre-employment screening is the process of verifying the credentials of a job applicant to ensure that they meet predetermined job qualifications and requirements, that they are trustworthy and reliable and that they will fit into your company culture. Employment screening checks can range from DBS and Right to Work checks to adverse and social media checks.

Who is it for?

Anyone! Organisations of all kinds would benefit from increased confidence in the suitability of their hire.
